# Privacy Policy

**Studio AI — by Maxnovate**
Last updated: May 2025

Maxnovate Consultancy Company Limited ("Maxnovate", "we", "us") operates Studio AI at [studio.maxnovate.com](https://studio.maxnovate.com). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data we collect

### 1.1 Account data
When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and organisation details via Clerk, our authentication provider.

### 1.2 Payment data
When you pay via MTN Mobile Money, we collect your phone number and transaction reference. We do not store your MoMo PIN or full account credentials.

### 1.3 Usage data
We collect data about how you use Studio AI, including:
- Prompts and inputs you submit for generation
- Generated outputs and job history
- Feature usage and session activity
- Device type, browser, and approximate location (country level)

### 1.4 Voice data
If you use voice cloning features, we process voice samples you upload. These are stored securely and used only to deliver the voice generation service within your account.

### 1.5 Uploaded content
Files and media you upload are stored on Cloudflare R2 and processed solely to deliver the requested generation.

---

## 2. How we use your data

We use your data to:

- Provide, operate, and improve Studio AI
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Send transactional emails (receipts, account notifications)
- Respond to support requests
- Monitor for abuse and enforce our Terms of Service
- Comply with legal obligations under Ugandan law

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 3. Data sharing

We share data only with the following third-party services necessary to operate Studio AI:

| Provider | Purpose | Data shared |
|---|---|---|
| Clerk | Authentication | Name, email |
| Cloudflare R2 | File storage | Uploaded files, generated outputs |
| MTN Mobile Money | Payment processing | Phone number, transaction amount |
| Polar | Subscription billing | Email, plan details |
| Vercel | Hosting | Request logs |
| Neon / PostgreSQL | Database | All structured data |

Each provider operates under their own privacy policy. We ensure they provide adequate data protection.

---

## 4. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

- Account data is deleted within 30 days
- Generated content and uploaded files are deleted within 90 days
- Payment records are retained for 7 years as required by Ugandan tax law

---

## 5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
- Encrypted storage for sensitive data at rest
- Access controls limiting data access to authorised personnel
- Regular security reviews

No system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at [info@maxnovate.com](mailto:info@maxnovate.com).

---

## 6. Your rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate personal data
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Export** your generated content and job history
- **Object** to processing of your data where it relies on our legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [info@maxnovate.com](mailto:info@maxnovate.com). We will respond within 30 days.

---

## 7. Cookies

Studio AI uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

---

## 8. Children

Studio AI is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 9. International data transfers

Our infrastructure is hosted primarily in the United States (Vercel, Cloudflare) and the European Union. By using Studio AI, you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in these jurisdictions, which may have different data protection laws than Uganda.

---

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or by posting a notice on the platform. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
